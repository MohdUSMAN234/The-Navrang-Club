A note from The Vice President

As a Vice President, I pledge to uphold the values of inclusivity, respect, and unity that define our club. I envision a year filled with engaging events, thought-provoking discussions, and meaningful collaborations that deepen our understanding of each other's cultures.

As your vice president, I am committed to ensuring that our club remains a welcoming space for all, where every member feels valued, respected, and empowered to share their cultural heritage.

Together, let us embark on a journey of exploration, learning, and connection. Together, let us continue to make our Cultural Club a beacon of diversity and understanding

Our cultural club is more than just a gathering of like-minded individuals; it is a platform for dialogue, learning, and celebration. It is a place where we not only embrace our own heritage but also embrace and respect the heritage of others. It is through this mutual respect and understanding that we foster a sense of unity and belonging that transcends borders and differences.

Thank you for entrusting me with this role, and I look forward to working alongside each and every one of you to make our cultural club an even stronger force for unity and inclusion in our community.

Muskan Sinha Vice President, The Navrang Club PSIT
