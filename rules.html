Event Rules

  1. Each team will be assigned a Team Coordinator. They are here to help you and ensure fair play. Please respect their guidance.
  2. Any team found engaging in malpractice will be immediately disqualified. Let's keep it honest and fun for everyone.
  3. All team members can select their Team Captain. Choose wisely, as they will lead you to victory!
  4. All mobile phones and electronic devices must be submitted to the core team before the event starts. Failure to do so will result in team elimination.
  5. Embrace the theme of Money Heist! Each team should pick and announce a team name based on the cities of India.
  6. Only the Team Captain is allowed to keep one mobile phone during the event. This will be essential for communication and clues.
  7. The hunt will take you through various locations, ranging from G Block to AA Block, except for one special question.
  8. Play responsibly and take care of the college property. We want to leave everything as we found it.
  9. Maintain the decorum of the event at all times. Respect the rules, the coordinators, and your fellow participants.
  10. Participants are not allowed to interact with the audience during the event. Focus on the game and your team's strategy.
